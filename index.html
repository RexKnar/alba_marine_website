Our Services

Marine Engineering & Technical Services

We provide drydock supervision, in-voyage repairs, condition assessments, and turnkey retrofits. Our in-house engineers are equipped to manage both mechanical and structural tasks in compliance with class standards.

Vessel Operations & Support

From crew management to logistics, voyage planning to onboard inspections, we ensure vessels remain compliant and mission-ready at all times.

Ship Chartering & Brokerage

Offering a fleet of offshore support vessels (OSVs), tugs, barges, and supply ships. We also manage sales and purchases of vessels, acting as reliable intermediaries in complex negotiations.

Tank Cleaning & Sanitation

We provide tank cleaning services for oil, water, and ballast tanks using automated or manual methods—ensuring environmental compliance and safety.

Marine Logistics & Warehousing

Strategically located shore-based storage facilities to support seamless loading, spares management, and consolidated cargo operations.

Equipment Rental & Maintenance

Access a comprehensive catalog of marine equipment—cranes, compressors, hoses, and firefighting tools—with full inspection and maintenance included.
